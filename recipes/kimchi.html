Corned Beef Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

A savory, spicy, and tangy dish with a combination of seasoned beef and fermented kimchi stir-fried with rice. Use a nonstick skillet for the rice. A fast cooked recipe with deep and profound flavor.

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 35 mins Servings: 2 Yield: 2 large bowls

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups kimchi, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups diced fully cooked corned beef (or bacon)
  • 3 cups cold cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onion
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoon oil, or no taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Drain kimchi over a pot, reserving the juice.
  2. Meanwhile, coat a pan with olive oil and add corned beef.
  3. Chop kimchi into small chunks; add to the pan. Place pan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until mixture is browned to your liking, 4 to 7 minutes. Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang.
  4. Mix rice mixture together and spread evenly over the pan; cook for 3 to 4 minutes. Mix and pat rice down again. Continue cooking, mixing and pressing rice every 3 to 4 minutes, until evenly browned and crusty, 12 to 15 minutes total.
  5. Mix green onion, sesame seeds, and sesame oil into the fried rice. Taste and season with soy sauce. Stir again. Transfer fried rice to serving bowls.
